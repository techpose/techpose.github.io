🐝 Environment

Bee Rescue Management System

Challenge

Bee rescue teams across regions lacked structured systems to log rescue requests, dispatch the nearest available rescuer, and maintain historical data on hive relocations and colony health — leading to delayed responses and lost environmental records.

Solution

Techpose built a full-stack mobile and web rescue coordination platform with role-based access for field rescuers, team leads, and administrators. The app enables real-time rescue request logging, live rescuer assignment, geo-tagged hive records, and a rich analytics dashboard tracking rescue trends, response times, and colony outcomes over time.

Flutter NodeJS MongoDB
Significantly faster rescuer dispatch and response time
Geo-tagged hive relocation records for environmental research
Analytics dashboard tracking colony health trends
Structured data supporting conservation reporting
🌏 World's First
🪡 Cultural Tech

Gullakaari

Challenge

India's rich tradition of handcrafted art was losing reach and relevance — artisans lacked a digital storefront, and buyers had no way to discover or customise traditional crafts at scale.

Solution

Techpose built Gullakaari — a marketplace and e-learning platform dedicated to traditional Indian crafts, connecting artisans directly with buyers. At its core is the AI-Artisan model: users select any Indian art form and a product type, and Gullakaari's AI generates a fully customised design which is then hand-crafted by verified Gullakaari artisans — making it the world's first AI-powered customisation engine in the cultural tech space.

MERN Stack AI / LLM Generative AI E-commerce
World's first AI-powered craft customisation engine
Digital marketplace connecting artisans with global buyers
E-learning platform preserving traditional art knowledge
Artisan livelihoods supported through direct commerce
